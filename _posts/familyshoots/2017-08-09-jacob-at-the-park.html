Bubs aka Baby J aka JJ aka Jacob Macko enjoyed a fun evening at the park with Mama, Gofs and Piya, while Dada was at office. He got to run, jump, squeal, slide, swing and scrape his palms and knees... So, in his world, this was a pretty successful outing.

To quote the wise Baby J himself, “I wanna go to park again. Baby likes park. Baby went zoom. Baby fell down. Baby wants to go ducky ducky. Baby wants grass. I don't want grass. I want Mama. Tiiki taaka toot toot. Baby sat on baby chair. Lesssgoo again Mama. No going home.”
