The rains played hide and seek for a couple of days prior to the photoshoot, but thanks to the sky gods, we were gifted with brilliant weather towards the second half of the day. Guruprasad, a school mate of mine was back in Goa after a while, and we were meeting after more than 15 years! Instead of books this time, we had our wives along, and Guru brought along a sweet little daughter too!

We did a bit of catching up and had a nice pleasant evening at the beach. Here are some photos from that evening in Reis Magos, Goa.
