On May 7, 2011 at Panjim, Goa, Marie-dale (Md) and Rohit decided to walk down the aisle. The wedding was at the Chapel of Mount Mary, atop a hill, overlooking old Goa.

The bride gifted the groom a motorbike and the lovebirds decided to ditch the wedding car to ride back, (as I chased along), to the reception venue on a boat afloat the Mandovi river. A perfect sunset gave them company.

Fun fact: Md was the first bride to contact me after seeing some of Evelyn and Ryan's wedding photos. This was my first real "assignment". Thanks for setting the stone rolling.
