Dec 2, 2012 at Saligao/Velsao, Goa. The day when Ninella Fernandes and Elston Dennis decided to walk down the aisle and walk back out later as husband and wife. From Ninella’s quiet home in Saligao to a grand celebration at Coco Loco later that night it was a day (and night) to remember.
