December 19, 2011 at Panjim, Goa.

Abigail and Tushar traveled over a 1000 miles from USA to scenic Goa to finally say “I Do” after being in a committed relationship for a good 4 years. The nuptial was at the Our Lady Church, Siridao.

Fun fact: This picture that captures the newlyweds and their friends in their elements, was shortlisted for the Best Photograph under the “Friends and Family” section at the “Better Photography – Wedding Photographer of the Year 2011” awards. I did not win. :P
