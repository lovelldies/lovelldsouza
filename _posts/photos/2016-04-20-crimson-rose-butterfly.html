Found this little chap flying around, while I was waiting for my clients a wedding venue, on a nice sunny evening in Goa.

Pachliopta hector, also know as the Crimson Rose, is a butterfly from Australasia / Indomalaya ecozone (Australia), with a wingspan or around 10 centimeters. It is a member of the family Papiloinidae (also known as Swallowtail).
