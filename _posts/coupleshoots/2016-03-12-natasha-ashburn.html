Natasha decided to surprise her husband Ashburn on a summer day, when they were visiting their motherland (Goa). She conspired with me to arrange for a clandestine photo shoot. I had never met them, but thanks to some photos she shared, I was to spot them when they reach the Ashwem beach for a “casual evening out” and capture candid images of them like a stalker. With my wife for company, to make my bearded self look less suspicious, we followed them as they walked along the beach, capturing their moments together. After stalking them for a while, when things started getting too suspicious, Natasha broke the surprise and we went on to get some more shots of the happy couple on the quiet beach. An evening, well-spent.
