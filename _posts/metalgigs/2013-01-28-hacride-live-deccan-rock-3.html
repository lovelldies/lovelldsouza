The French metal band, Hacride performing live at 10 Lounge, Secunderabad, India. This was their first stop as part of their Deccan Rock III India tour which would be covering multiple cities. They shared the stage with Skrypt and Perpetual Void (from Hyderabad) and Nothnegal (from Maldives).
