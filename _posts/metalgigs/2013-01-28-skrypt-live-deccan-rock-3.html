Hyderabadi metal band, Skrypt performing live at 10 Lounge, Secunderabad, India. This was their first stop as part of their Deccan Rock III India tour which would be covering multiple cities. They shared the stage with Perpetual Void (from Hyderabad), Nothnegal (from Maldives) and Hacride (from France).
