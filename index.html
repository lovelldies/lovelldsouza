Let me bore you by

Talking about myself

I'm a wedding photographer based in Goa. I was born here and lived a major chunk of my life walking around the streets of Mapusa & Parra. So, if you want to know the quickest route from the Mapusa Court to Alankar, you can ask me.

A not-so-long time ago, I had a full-time job in Hyderabad. One fine day, the people higher up in the food chain lost their bearings and decided to promote me. Hence, like all brainless millennials out there, I decided to go out and buy a point & shoot camera. But I got a little over-ambitious and bought a Canon 1000D. Friends and family allowed me to shoot their weddings for fun. And then, later on, some ambitious people started paying me money to shoot their weddings. And, that my friends, is how the Canon 1000D landed up in the back of the cupboard.

Okay, enough of this talking nonsense. I've bored myself and I don't want to bore you too. Just scroll, click around & feel free to browse through my work. Don't feel shy to hit me up via my contact page, if you aren't one of those scrolling types.
