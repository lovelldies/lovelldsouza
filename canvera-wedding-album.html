Here is where your wedding memories will finally be stored. Your grandkids may puke on it, but you will able to wipe the puke off and continue viewing the photos without any issue. To give you some stats - the book is 12 inches high and wide. It is pretty light to lug around, and can also be used as a cutting board if required. But yeah, you would not want to do that. Your grandmother will love looking at it. If you don't have a living grandmother, just show it to some random old lady and seek her approval.
